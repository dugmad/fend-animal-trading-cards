Giant Panda

The World Wildlife Foundation's logo is based on the Giant Panda.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoeuca
  • Average Weight: 220 to 254 lb
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: The mountain ranges of Central China

Giant Pandas are identified by the patches of black around their eyes. Their diet consists of 99% bamboo. They need to eat upwards of 30 pounds of bamboo daily just to stay full. While the average lifespan in the wild is 20 years, Pandas in captivity can live as long as 30 years.
